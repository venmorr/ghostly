Ghosts can be classified into different types. Below are listed some common ghost types you may encounter.

Ghosts can be classified into different danger levels, ranging from 1 (non-threat) to the extremely rare DANGER LEVEL ZERO. If you encounter a DLZ (Danger Level Zero) do not waist time entering them into the system. Just run.

Apparition:

Apparitions are the most common type of ghosts, often resembling the deceased in appearance.

Danger Level: Low. They typically don't interact with the living and are more like residual energy imprints.

Poltergeist:

Poltergeists are known for their ability to move objects and create physical disturbances.

Danger Level: Moderate. They can be mischievous or disruptive but are rarely harmful.

Shadow People:

Shadow people are dark, shadowy figures often seen out of the corner of one's eye.

Danger Level: Low to Moderate. Some report feelings of dread, but physical harm is rare.

Banshee:

Banshees are female spirits known for wailing and foretelling death in some cultures.

Danger Level: Moderate. Their appearance is typically seen as an omen of death.

Doppelganger:

Doppelgangers are apparitions that mimic the living, often causing confusion and fear.

Danger Level: Moderate. Seeing one's own doppelganger is often seen as an ill omen.

Residual Haunting:

Residual hauntings involve repetitive actions or events from the past that replay without consciousness.

Danger Level: Low. They're more like recordings and seldom interact with the living.

Vengeful Spirit:

These are spirits seeking revenge or justice for a wrongdoing in life.

Danger Level: High. They can be hostile and may try to harm the living to achieve their goals.

Spirit Attachments:

These are spirits that attach themselves to living individuals, often draining their energy or influencing their behavior.

Danger Level: Moderate. The impact can vary from mild discomfort to more severe possession.

Child Ghost:

The spirits of deceased children or infants, often appearing in a childlike form.

Danger Level: Low. They are usually harmless or seeking comfort.

Elemental Spirits:

Elemental spirits are associated with the natural elements like earth, water, fire, and air.

Danger Level: Varies. Some may be benign, while others can be unpredictable or hostile depending on the element.
